Analysis of Spree and Mass Shootings in America

A spree shooting is classified as a shooting which has victims in multiple locations with little gap between incidents.

A mass shooting is classified as a shooting which has more than 4 victims, excluding the shooter(s) in a single location.

We can conclude that, of the data we analyzed, over 90% of shootings are carried out by males who obtained weapons legally more than 80% of the time.
